## Step 1: File LLC (CA SOS) — ~30 minutes

### Getting to the right place

**Important**: The bizfile site has two main areas — **Search** (look up existing businesses) and **File** (create new filings). To form a new LLC, you must use **File**, not Search.

1. Go to [bizfile.sos.ca.gov](https://bizfile.sos.ca.gov) (or [bizfileOnline.sos.ca.gov](https://bizfileOnline.sos.ca.gov))
2. **Sign in** with your account (created with sankalp@paperworklabs.com)
3. In the top menu, click **"File"** (not "Search")
4. Under **File**, select **"LLC"** → **"Articles of Organization - CA LLC"**
5. You'll go through a multi-step wizard. Work through each step in order.

### Step-by-step: What to enter at each screen

| Step | What it means | What to enter |
|------|---------------|---------------|
| **Privacy Warning / Terms** | Legal disclaimer | Check the box to accept, continue |
| **Submitter** | Contact person for this filing (optional but recommended) | **Name**: Sankalp Sharma<br>**Email**: sankalp@paperworklabs.com<br>**Phone**: Your phone number<br>**Extension**: Leave blank |
| **No Professional Services** | Confirms you're not using a registered agent service for this filing | Check the box (you're filing yourself) |
| **Limited Liability Company Name** | The legal name of your LLC | **Paperwork Labs LLC** |
| **Business Addresses** | Where the LLC is located | Your home address (CA requires a California address). Use the same address for principal office and mailing if you have no separate office. |
| **Agent for Service of Process** | Who receives legal papers for the LLC | **Your name** (Sankalp Sharma) + **your home address** — you can be your own RA initially. Switch to a commercial RA (Northwest, CorpNet) later when LaunchFree ships. |
| **Purpose, Management and File Date** | Business purpose and who runs it | **Purpose**: Any lawful purpose<br>**Management**: Manager-managed (you are the sole manager)<br>**Organizer**: Sankalp Sharma, sankalp@paperworklabs.com<br>**File date**: Today's date |
| **Attachments** | Optional supporting docs | Usually none needed for a standard LLC |
| **Review and Signature** | Final review | Review all entries, sign electronically |
| **Processing Fees** | $70 filing fee | Pay by credit card |
| **File Document** | Submit | Click to file. **Save the confirmation number and file number immediately.** |

### Submitter info — do I add my name?

**Yes.** The Submitter section is for contact about this specific filing. Enter:
- **Name**: Your full legal name (Sankalp Sharma)
- **Email**: sankalp@paperworklabs.com
- **Phone**: Your phone number

These fields are optional, but the Secretary of State may use them to contact you about the filing. Using your name and company email is correct.

### After filing

- **Confirmation**: You'll get a confirmation number and file number. Save both.
- **Stamped Articles**: Download the stamped PDF when it becomes available (often same day or next business day).
- **Timeline**: Often same-day or next business day. Check the CA SOS status page if needed.

## Step 2: Apply for EIN — ~15 minutes

1. Wait until you have the CA SOS confirmation (LLC is officially formed)
2. Go to [irs.gov/e-file-providers](https://www.irs.gov/businesses/small-businesses-self-employed/apply-for-an-employer-identification-number-ein-online) — or search "IRS EIN apply online"
3. Select **LLC** as entity type
4. Select **Domestic** (US)
5. Enter LLC details:
   - Legal name: Paperwork Labs LLC
   - Address
   - Responsible party: You (Sankalp), SSN, address
6. **EIN is issued immediately** — save the PDF confirmation

---

## Step 3: Get PTIN — ~15 minutes, $18.75

1. Go to [rpr.irs.gov](https://rpr.irs.gov)
2. Create account (name + email — use sankalp@paperworklabs.com)
3. You'll receive a temporary password via email
4. Verify identity via **ID.me** (photo ID + selfie) — required
5. Complete application:
   - Personal info (name, SSN, DOB, address)
   - Prior year tax return info (name, address, filing status from your most recent return)
   - Business info: Paperwork Labs LLC, address, phone
6. Pay $18.75 (credit/debit card, non-refundable)
7. **PTIN issued immediately** — save the number (you'll need it for EFIN)

**Note**: PTIN expires December 31 each year. Renew annually.

## Step 4: Create IRS e-Services Account — ~15 minutes

1. Go to [irs.gov/e-file-providers/e-services-online-tools-for-tax-professionals](https://www.irs.gov/e-file-providers/e-services-online-tools-for-tax-professionals)
2. Click **Register** for e-Services
3. Verify identity via ID.me (may already be done from PTIN step)
4. Complete registration — use sankalp@paperworklabs.com
5. Save your credentials securely

## Step 5: Submit EFIN Application — ~30-45 minutes

1. Log into IRS e-Services
2. Navigate to **e-file Application** (or "e-file Provider Application")
3. **Select provider types** — check ALL that apply:
   - **Software Developer** — you're building tax software
   - **Transmitter** — you'll transmit returns to IRS
   - **Online Provider** — you're offering online filing to consumers
   - **Electronic Return Originator (ERO)** — you can originate returns for clients
4. **Firm information**:
   - Firm name: Paperwork Labs LLC
   - EIN: (from Step 2)
   - Business address
   - Business phone
   - Email: sankalp@paperworklabs.com
5. **Principal and Responsible Official**:
   - You (Sankalp) as both Principal and Responsible Official
   - SSN, DOB, address
   - PTIN (from Step 3)
6. **Save progress** — you can return and complete later if needed
7. **Submit** when all sections are complete

**You can save and return** — the application doesn't have to be completed in one session.

---

## Step 6: Schedule Fingerprinting (Livescan)

Since you're not a CPA, attorney, or enrolled agent, you need electronic fingerprinting:

1. After submitting the EFIN application, the IRS will send instructions (or check your e-Services inbox)
2. Find an IRS-authorized Livescan vendor: [irs.gov/e-file-providers/become-an-authorized-e-file-provider](https://www.irs.gov/e-file-providers/become-an-authorized-e-file-provider)
3. **No cost** — IRS covers the fingerprinting fee
4. Schedule appointment as soon as possible — delays in fingerprinting delay approval

## Step 7: IRS Suitability Check — 4-6 weeks

The IRS will conduct:
- Credit check
- Tax compliance check
- Criminal background check
- Prior e-file non-compliance review

If everything is clean, you'll receive your **EFIN** and **Software Developer ID** in 4-6 weeks (up to 45 days).
