**What Olga should do:** Think about a real deal she lost at Contentful or Khoros. If this story matches a real experience, she can use it. If not, she should swap in her actual lost deal.

## Things NOT to Say (Quick Reference)

- Don't say "GPT-4o" or "GPT-4." Current models are GPT-5, GPT-5 mini, GPT-5.4.
- Don't claim fine-tuning works on GPT-5. It's only on GPT-4.1 and GPT-4.1 mini.
- Don't say "I'm not technical" without immediately following with a technical point.
- Don't badmouth Meta. Frame the departure positively.
- Don't badmouth competitors (Anthropic, Google). Say "OpenAI has a strong advantage here."
- Don't quote exact dollar amounts for OpenAI deal sizes. Say "multi-million dollar."
- Don't confuse Whisper (recorded audio transcription) with Realtime API (live streaming audio).
- Don't oversell. Say "based on comparable deployments" not "this will definitely work."
- Don't use buzzwords: "leverage," "synergy," "robust," "seamless," "transformative."
